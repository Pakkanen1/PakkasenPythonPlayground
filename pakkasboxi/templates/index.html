Adventure catalog

The Spiral Prince Ascends

Tower crawl for characters of lvl 4-6, designed for Shadowdark RPG. Here players get to explore a wizard's tower on top of a cold mountain where something has gone terribly wrong.

Dungeon crawl for characters of lvl 1-3, designed for Shadowdark RPG. This is a delve into an ancient dwarven forge where a weapon that could slay a god was once forged. Integrates nicely with the "Isles of Andrik" setting by the Arcane Library.
